Stilltone

Privacy Policy · Effective 10 June 2026

Stilltone is an ambient sound app designed around a simple principle: what you listen to is nobody's business but yours.

What we collect

Nothing. Stilltone collects no data of any kind.

  • No accounts or sign-ups
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising or advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs or services
  • No network connections — the app works entirely offline

What stays on your device

Stilltone keeps no record of your activity at all. The app doesn't save your sound choices, volumes, or timer settings — every session starts fresh by design. Nothing is written to your device beyond the app itself, and nothing ever leaves it.

Permissions

Stilltone requests no permissions. It does not access your microphone, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information. Audio playback in the background and on the lock screen uses standard iOS media features that require no access to your data.

Children

Stilltone is suitable for all ages. Because the app collects no data, no children's data is collected either.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of Stilltone ever changes any of the above, this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the change will be noted in the App Store release notes.
